Our Tailoring Process

1

Consultation

We discuss your needs, preferences, and take precise measurements.

2

Design & Fabric

You select from our fabric collection and finalize the design.

3

Pattern Making

We create custom patterns based on your measurements.

4

Fitting Session

We do a fitting with the unfinished garment for adjustments.

5

Final Delivery

Your perfectly fitted garment is ready for collection.

  • Machine operation and maintenance
  • Taking measurements
  • Pattern drafting and cutting
  • Sewing techniques for different fabrics
  • Garment construction
  • Business management for tailors
